This is an interesting, and somewhat ongoing data story presented by Aljazeera. One of the most interesting things about this story 
is that while the original publication date is November 2025, it is still being updated, with the most recent update coming 15 hours 
ago at the time of this writing.

This story uses a combination of graphics to illustrate the violence that Israel is continuing to exercise in the Gaza Strip, while simultaneouslylaunching attacks with the US against Iran. According to the story, since the ceasefire agreement brokered by President Trump in October of 2025, there have been at least 2,037 violations, and only 22 days out of 169 where Israel has not launched an attack on Gaza. These attacks have also resulted in at least 691 Palestinian deaths, and 1876 injuries. In total since October 7, 2023, Israeli attacks have resulted in at least 72,267 Palestinian deaths throughout the Gaza territory. The story displays this data with a calendar showing days that Gaza has been attacked,a graph showing the number of individuals injured or killed over time, and a graphic displaying the total deaths and injuries as map points.
